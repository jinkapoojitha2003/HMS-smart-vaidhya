Blood Donation

A single drop of your blood could be a drop of life for someone else

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a simple, safe, and rewarding act. Donating blood can help save lives and ensure that patients receive the critical care they need. Here are some reasons to donate:

  • Save Lives: One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Help Patients: Blood is needed in emergency situations, surgeries, and for chronic conditions.
  • Free Health Check: Every donor gets a free health checkup before donating.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for donating blood, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years old and in good health.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Have no history of hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, or other infectious diseases.
  • Not have donated blood in the last 56 days.
